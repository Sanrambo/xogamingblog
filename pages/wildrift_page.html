League of Legends: Wild Rift Open Beta Coming to MENA

Players will have the support of local servers in Bahrain.

Riot Games chas announced that the open beta for League of Legends: Wild Rift will be available for the MENA region from 10th December.

The mobile online multi-player game will be available on both iOS and Android devices, and will support local servers based in Bahrain. Alongside the release, a new content patch will also be issued that will introduce several new champions and skins, balance adjustments, bugfixes, as well as some player-requested features.

“We are really excited to be launching Open Beta for League of Legends: Wild Rift across the MENA region,” said Karim Hachani, Head of Publishing at Riot Games MENA, in a press statement. “We know there a lot of gamers around the world especially in the region who are intrigued about League of Legends:Wild Rift and on 10 December, the wait will be over as they will be able to get their first experience of playing.”


Riot Games also plans to feature complete Arabic localization for the game, which will be available sometime next year.


Along with the MENA region, the game will launch in open beta across Europe, Russia & CIS, and Turkey.
